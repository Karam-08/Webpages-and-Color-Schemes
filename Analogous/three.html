    About Us

Welcome to Jimmy's Turkey Farm! Our farm serves fresh turkeys every Thanksgiving!

We sell many Turkeys that are frozen and on the go! Our Turkeys come Pre-Seasoned, and you can include other sides like Mashed Potatoes for some extra cash. The Turkeys are tended with love and care until they are ripe enough for slaughter. Then, we season the meat with our famous Lemon Pepper or Cajun seasoning. Really, it's all up to you. You can even get one unseasoned!
